# Certification Training

New engineers may be required to complete certification training before being allowed to perform selected technical tasks.

The onboarding process should include required training modules, knowledge checks, practical exercises, supervisor review and a final certification decision.

AI can help generate learning paths and quizzes, but it should not independently certify an employee.

Certification approval must remain a human decision.

If an employee is not sure whether a task requires certification, the employee should ask a supervisor or training coordinator before performing the task.
