# Quality Procedures

Industrial employees must understand internal quality procedures before working independently.

Important topics include quality control checkpoints, documentation accuracy, non-conformance reporting, traceability of production steps, inspection requirements and escalation paths for quality issues.

If incorrect inspection documentation is found, the employee should stop using the affected record, report the issue to a supervisor or quality expert and follow the non-conformance procedure.

Quality-related decisions should be based on approved procedures and verified documentation.

AI-generated explanations can support learning, but final interpretation of quality standards should remain with qualified experts.
