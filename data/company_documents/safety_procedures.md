# Safety Procedures

New technical employees must complete safety onboarding before working near industrial equipment.

Key safety topics include personal protective equipment, lockout tagout procedures, emergency evacuation rules, working near high-voltage systems, reporting incidents and near misses, and safe operation of specialized industrial tools.

Employees must not perform safety-critical tasks without approval from an experienced supervisor or safety expert.

For maintenance activities, employees must verify that equipment has been stopped, isolated and secured according to the approved lockout tagout process.

If a procedure is unclear, the employee must ask a domain expert before taking action.

AI-generated explanations are only learning support and must not replace official procedures, supervisor approval or expert decisions.
